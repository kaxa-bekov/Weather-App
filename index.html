Hold On, Hold On

Please read the following introduction to have an idea on how to use the app and what its functionallity is.

This app takes one input, that is any location in the world and shows weather data as well as some of the air conditions for the given location.

In the left top corner you can see a dialog asking to allow your location, this is only to populate the app with weather information of your current location.

Please allow the locatation access.

This App features 3 different tabs to dislay the data and one "Settings" tab to customize the app for your own preferences.

  1. Weather Tab

    This tab shows temperature summary, hourly and daily forecasts, as well as air conditions for the given location.

    Please click the "See more" button to expand the air conditions.

  2. Cities Tab

    In the "Cities" tab you can add as many different locations as you would like and see their weather information.

    By clicking on any of the list items you will select that location as your main location and the "Weather" tab will reflect the data for that particular location.

    You can remove the locations from the list by Double Clicking them.

  3. Map Tab

    Here you can see a Google Map that will add markers for all of the locations that you have added on this particular tab as well as their weather data in a window above the location marker.

    The informational window with weather info is still under development and may behave unexpectedly sometimes. In order to reopen a widnow after you have closed it simply click on the marker on the map.

    You will also notice that you can add and remove locations from your list in the "Map" tab as well as the "Cities" tab. They are fully synchronized.

  4. Settings Tab

    And finally in the "Settings" tab you are able to select different measurment units and set time format that is more comfortable for you.

    Please note that the "Notifications", "Subscription" and "Sign Up" feature are not implemented in the beta version.

    After you have selected desired measurment units and hour format you can go back to "Weather" tab and see that it had changed accordingly.

Please let me know what do you think and what are the possible improvements taht can be made. Any feedback is greatly appreciated!!!
